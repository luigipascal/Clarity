How It Works

1

Add your AI key

Go to Settings and add your OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), or Google (Gemini) API key. Your key is stored locally in your browser - we never see it.

2

Define your goal

Start with the Prerequisite Mapper. Enter what you're trying to achieve and let AI break it down into foundations.

3

Surface blind spots

Use Assumptions to find hidden beliefs. Use Inverse Brief to define constraints. Use Collisions to spark new ideas.

4

Export your clarity

Generate an AI summary report, export to Markdown, or create a visual PDF. Share with your team or keep for reference.
