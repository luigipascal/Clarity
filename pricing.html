Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay for AI?

No. You use your own API key from OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), or Google (Gemini). You pay the AI provider directly - typically $0.01-0.05 per query. We don't charge anything for AI usage.

Is this a subscription?

No. All paid tiers are one-time payments. You get lifetime access to the features. Updates are included for 1 year (Starter/Pro) or lifetime (Agency).

What happens after 1 year of updates?

You keep using the app forever. After 1 year, you just won't receive new features unless you purchase again. Existing features continue to work.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes! You can upgrade from Free to any paid tier, or from Starter to Pro/Agency at any time. Just pay the difference.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your API keys are stored only in your browser - we never see them. With cloud sync, your project data is encrypted and stored securely. We don't sell or share your data.

Do I need an account to use the free version?

No. You can use all free features without creating an account. An account is only needed for cloud sync and other premium features.
